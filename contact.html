Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Small projects may take 4-6 weeks, while larger enterprise solutions can take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the consultation phase.

Do you work with startups?

Absolutely! We love working with startups and understand the unique challenges they face. We offer flexible engagement models and can scale our services as your business grows.

What industries do you serve?

We work across various industries including fintech, healthcare, e-commerce, SaaS, and more. Our technology expertise translates well across different sectors.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! We offer maintenance and support packages to ensure your solution continues to perform optimally. We're committed to long-term partnerships with our clients.

Can you work with our existing team?

Definitely! We can augment your existing team, provide technical leadership, or work as an independent development partner—whatever works best for your organization.

What's your development process?

We follow agile methodologies with regular sprints, demos, and feedback cycles. This ensures transparency, adaptability, and that the final product meets your expectations.
